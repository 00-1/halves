Goblin Gold: The Void Throne

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Goblin Gold is a free educational maths game for children. We built it to collect no personal data at all.

What we collect

Nothing. Goblin Gold does not ask for a name, email, age, or any account. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data stored on your device

Your progress, best times, settings, and collected in-game items are saved only in your device's local storage, on your device. This never leaves the device and is not sent to us or anyone else. Clearing the app's data (or uninstalling) removes it.

No tracking, no ads, no third parties

Goblin Gold contains no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. We do not profile users or share data with anyone.

Network use

The app downloads its own program files to run, and checks a small version file (build.json) to know when an update is available. These requests do not include any personal data. The app makes no third-party requests — all fonts and assets are self-hosted; there is no advertising, analytics, or third-party SDK.

Hosting and distribution

The app is delivered over the web from GitHub Pages and distributed through Google Play. As with any website or app store, these providers' servers may log standard technical information (such as IP address, device type, and request time) as part of serving and distributing the app, under their own privacy policies (see GitHub's and Google's privacy policies). We do not access, receive, or store this information, and the app itself collects no data.

Children's privacy

Goblin Gold is intended for children and complies with COPPA and UK/EU GDPR-K by collecting no personal data whatsoever.
