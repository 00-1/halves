# Play Store listing copy — Goblin Gold: The Void Throne

**App name (≤30):** Goblin Gold: The Void Throne

**Short description (≤80):**
Fast 11+ mental-maths drills — tables, fractions, % — as a pixel RPG. No ads.

**Full description (≤4000):**
Goblin Gold turns 11+ mental-maths practice into a fast, friendly pixel game. Short timed drills build the building blocks children actually need for the exam — and an RPG layer (collect, level up, battle) keeps them coming back.

What it drills
• Halves & doubles · times tables · squares & cubes
• Fractions & percentages of an amount · fraction↔decimal↔percentage
• Number bonds · place value · add & subtract · rounding
• BODMAS · factors, multiples & primes · Roman numerals · negative numbers
• Reasoning: proportion & scaling, ratio sharing, averages (mean/median/mode/range), LCM/HCF, time intervals, speed–distance–time, area, volume & angles, function machines, "complete the sum"

Why it works
• Speed + accuracy: bite-size rounds train recall to automaticity — the difference between knowing it and knowing it fast enough under exam pressure.
• Mastery, not pressure: gentle timers, mastery-gated unlocks, celebratory progress — built to avoid maths anxiety.
• A reason to return: earn collectibles and heroes, take on Arena battles — practice that feels like play.

Made for families
• No ads. No in-app purchases. No accounts. No tracking. Everything stays on the device.
• Works offline after the first load. Installs like an app.

Built for UK 11+ (GL-style) maths, useful for any child strengthening mental arithmetic.
